About this site.

Before facebook. Before google was the first stop on any web browsing session. Before instagram, twitter and , there was just the web.

Most of the time, you didn't know where you were, who owned the site or what the hell was going on. Maybe you just followed a link from a friend's "links" page. Maybe you'd stumbled across the page because you were 15 pages deep in a search engine (yes it used to be that hard to find stuff back then).

I wanted this site to be somewhere one could explore. It would contain a wide variety of images, words etc. and one could get lost for a while. You might stumble onto something as if searching through a second hand shop or an attic. I hope you enjoy.

Adam.
