Someplace Nice

Hello, my name is Adam. You may also know me as Red. I'm an artist and engineer.

I like to make things

I have made, or helped make a wide variety of things over the course of my life. I have made digital democracy tools with Delib. They are a great company. I have also worked on open source projects, including plone.api.

I also make art. Mostly paintings. You can see some of them at redrickstevens.com. I used to take a lot of photographs. I guess I would describe what I used to do as "street photography". You can see some of my favourites on Flickr.

At University, I studied Music and Visual Art. I made a lot of music during this period of my life. I'm currrently releasing music under the moniker Null Horizon. You can hear a few of my older pieces on SoundCloud. This also includes a piece by the band Carl White, of which I was an original member.

I've recently been working on a computer game. This is an audio adventure game set in the future. It's still in development, at the moment, but hopefully soon it will be playable through the web.

Back in the physical world, I also recently finished a project to build a Cabin. The interior still needs finishing, but I'm really happy with how it turned out.
