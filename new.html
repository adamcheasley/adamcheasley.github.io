I have made, or helped make a wide variety of things over the course of my life. I have made digital democracy tools with Delib. They are a great company. I have also worked on open source projects, including plone.api.

I also make art. Mostly paintings. You can see some of them at redrickstevens.com. I used to take a lot of photographs. I guess I would describe what I used to do as "street photography". You can see some of my favourites on Flickr.

At University, I studied Music and Visual Art. I made a lot of music during this period of my life. You can hear a few of my favourite pieces on SoundCloud. This also includes a piece by the band Carl White, of which I was an original member.
